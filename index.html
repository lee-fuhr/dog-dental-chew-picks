How we choose our picks

We do not run lab tests or timed chewing trials. Instead, our recommendations are built on three pillars: the consensus of published veterinary and dental studies, the collective experience of thousands of real owners in online communities and review platforms, and the long‑term feedback from veterinary professionals we interview. This approach highlights products that perform consistently over months of use, not just in a single promo video. We also track common complaints, like chews that crumble too quickly, cause digestive upset, or lose their appeal after a few weeks. Products that earn a spot on our list have a clear majority of positive owner reports across a wide range of dog sizes, ages, and chewing styles.
